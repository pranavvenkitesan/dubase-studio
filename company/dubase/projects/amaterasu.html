Amaterasu

UI/UX Design & Website
Amaterasu is a physics-cognition lab transforming mental health. We designed a calm, premium website that turns a bold mission into a clear, welcoming first step.
Client:
Amaterasu
Industry:
Mental Health · Cognitive Tech
Services:
UI/UX Design, Web Design, Development
Timeline:
3 Weeks
Project Summary

Amaterasu is a physics-cognition lab working where technology meets nature to transform mental health. We designed a calm, premium website that turns a bold mission into a clear, welcoming first step.

  • A calm, trustworthy brand presence
  • A story-led, conversion-focused website
  • An effortless, intuitive journey
  • A scalable, reusable design system
  • Fast and responsive on every screen
Research & Strategy

We explored how people look for mental-health support their hesitations, their words, their needs. Those insights shaped a calm visual direction and one clear message: empowerment, not pressure.

UX Planning & Flow

We mapped the path from first curiosity to "Start your journey," then wireframed every step for clarity. Each screen gently guides the user forward with one obvious next action.

Final Outcome

A serene, high-performing website that makes a science-led mission feel human. Soft gradients, considered motion, and clear hierarchy build instant trust and calm.

Results & Impact

A focused, conversion-ready experience that deepens trust, lifts engagement, and gives Amaterasu a digital home built to grow.
