Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Dubase Studio provide?

We offer UI/UX design, web design & development, product design, brand identity, graphic design, and digital strategy services tailored to your business goals.

What industries do you work with?

We work with a wide range of industries including startups, technology, e-commerce, healthcare, fintech, education, and SaaS companies. Our flexible design approach allows us to adapt to the unique needs of different businesses.

What is your design process?

Our design process typically includes research, strategy, wireframing, UI design, prototyping, and final development. We focus on collaboration and user feedback at every stage to ensure the final product meets business goals and user needs.

How long does a project usually take?

Project timelines depend on the scope and complexity. A simple website may take 2–4 weeks, while larger projects such as full product design or web applications can take several months.

Can you redesign an existing website or product?

Yes, we specialize in redesigning existing websites and digital products. We analyze your current product, identify usability issues, and create a modern, improved design that enhances performance and user experience.
