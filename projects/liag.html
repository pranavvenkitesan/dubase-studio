Project Summary

Liag is an interior design studio based in Kerala that creates warm, considered homes and workplaces, from first concept through final styling. The goal of this project was to craft a calm, elegant brand identity and website that reflects their belief that a space should feel inevitable, rooted in how people want to live.

  • Build a warm, distinctive brand identity
  • Design an elegant, content-led website
  • Showcase residential, commercial & hospitality work
  • Make project browsing and discovery effortless
  • Ensure responsive & performance-optimized UI
Research & Strategy

We began by studying the studio's design philosophy, its audience of homeowners and businesses, and the wider interior design market. These insights shaped a refined visual direction and content strategy that puts light, material, and atmosphere at the centre of the brand story.

UX Planning & Flow

A structured UX approach mapped how visitors explore the studio's work, from services to individual projects. Clear information architecture and considered wireframes made it easy to browse portfolios across residential, commercial, hospitality, and styling, guiding visitors smoothly toward an enquiry.

Final Outcome

The final result is an elegant, atmospheric website paired with a cohesive brand identity. Generous imagery, calm typography, and thoughtful spacing let the interiors speak, giving Liag a refined digital presence that feels as considered as their spaces.

Results & Impact

The new experience strengthened brand perception, made the studio's portfolio easier to explore, and gave Liag a polished platform to attract residential, commercial, and hospitality clients.
